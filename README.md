# Glitter & Giggles

Welcome to Glitter & Giggles, your go-to destination for magical events and celebrations! We specialize in creating unforgettable experiences for birthdays, corporate events, cultural gatherings, and more.

## Features

- **Home**: Overview of our services and latest updates.
- **About**: Learn more about our team and mission.
- **Services**: Explore our range of event planning services including birthday parties, corporate events, cultural celebrations, inclusive events, school programs, and STEM workshops.
- **Gallery**: View photos from our past events.
- **Blog**: Read our latest tips and stories.
- **Contact**: Get in touch to plan your next event.

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript

## Getting Started

To view the website locally:

1. Clone the repository: `git clone https://github.com/brownjh18/Glitters.-.Giggles.git`
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser.
